ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Diego Revilla, I´m a Programmer and Designer in my free time, but I hope I can make a living of it in the future, my speciality are video games and simulations, where I can use my abilities in Programming, Design and Audio Creation to make a pretty complete work, but I also have knowledge and experience in Web Design and Mobile Apps

The  process

The Idea is always the first thing in the hole process, then it comes the sketching,  after that I can already work on it,  finally I polish it until it looks satisfying.

The tools

C++ is my most used language, but I also know Swift, for programming the tools I use the most are Visual Studio and Xcode, but I´m also familiar with Unity, Unreal and Zero, for design and art resources I use Photoshop, Blender and Krita among other Apps, and finally for music I use Ableton Live
